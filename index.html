Benefits

  1. The best news feed. You read only what is interesting and important for you, not for the media.
  2. Save time. Read the letter with a sampling of the best tweets, rather than the entire tape.
  3. Do not miss the steep tweets. Leafing through the tape may accidentally skip an interesting tweet with our letters this will not happen..
  4. You can influence the service. Service is just beginning to develop and grow, if you have ideas or suggestions - we will be happy to hear from you.
